## 🎯 About SAMWHA

**SAMWHA** (South Korea) is a well-known manufacturer of ceramic capacitors, including high voltage series used in:
- Consumer electronics (SMPS, adapters)
- LED lighting drivers
- Industrial power supplies
- Automotive electronics
- Telecom infrastructure
